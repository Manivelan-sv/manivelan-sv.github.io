More About Myself

I'm S.V.Manivelan

Research Scholar
Department of Physics,
M. R. Government Arts College (Affiliated to Bharathidasan University), Mannargudi, Tamilnadu, India - 614 001.

I am Manivelan Saminathan Vasuki, Research Scholar specializing in Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos, delving deep into Physics through computational insights.My research focuses on unraveling natural complexities using computational methods. I'm passionate about deciphering order in chaos, exploring emergent behaviors, and understanding nonlinear dynamics. I thrive on navigating these complexities, driven by curiosity to contribute to Computational Physics. I also study Extreme Value Theory, investigating statistical properties and dynamics of extreme events across various systems. By applying EVT principles, I aim to comprehend their occurrence and impact deeply. Guided by my mentor, Dr. I. Manimehan & Dr. K. Thamilmaran, provides invaluable mentorship, extending beyond academia, shaping my research journey. Motivated by an insatiable curiosity, I aim to contribute meaningfully to our research under their guidance.

I'm ready to engage in discussions and work, even without attribution. For me, research is all about discovering enjoyable new things and fostering enriching connections.
